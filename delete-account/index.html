Delete Your Expensely Pro Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Expensely Pro account and all associated data, you can request account deletion by following the instructions below.

How to request account deletion:

Send an email from the address associated with your account to:

support@expenselypro.com

Subject line: Delete my account

Once we receive your request, your account and all related data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 48 hours.

This includes:

  • Your profile information
  • Transactions and financial records
  • Projects and financial goals
  • Cloud backup data

Some limited information may be retained temporarily for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes as described in our privacy policy.
