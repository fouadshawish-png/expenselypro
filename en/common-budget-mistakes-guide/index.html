5 Common Budget Mistakes (And a 30-Day Fix Plan)

By Fouad Shawish · February 13, 2026

1) Monthly-only reviews

Monthly reviews are too late. Switch to a 10-minute weekly review and correct one category at a time.

2) Too many categories

Fewer categories = less friction. Keep 8–10 max.

3) Ignoring small recurring costs

Subscriptions and micro-charges add up. Track frequency weekly, not just totals.

4) Moving money from savings to cover drift

Lock savings and debt payments first. Adjust wants, not goals.

5) No buffer

Add a small weekly buffer for surprises instead of blowing up the plan.

Fix them in 30 days

  1. Week 1: Log everything; run first weekly review.
  2. Week 2: Cut categories to 10 and freeze savings transfers.
  3. Week 3: Add/adjust buffer; trim one subscription.
  4. Week 4: Refine one category and keep the weekly review habit.
