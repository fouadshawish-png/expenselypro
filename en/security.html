Security & privacy

Your privacy is a priority. Here is how we keep your data safe inside the app.

Data is stored on your device with local encryption to prevent unauthorized access.

App lock: enable PIN or biometrics to block access to your data.

Secure sync: when enabled, data is stored on trusted cloud services.

Minimal permissions: we request only what is needed for core features.

Data deletion: you can delete your data from within the app at any time.
