From daily expense logs to weekly budget corrections

Follow a 3-step flow: track expenses daily, review budgets weekly, and adjust debt paydown. Expect clarity on where money leaks within the first review cycle (7 days).

1) Create a wallet or account

Start with a cash wallet or bank account and set an accurate opening balance.

2) Log income and expenses immediately

Record every transaction in seconds with the right category and date.

3) Review reports and summaries

See monthly summaries and charts that show where your money goes.

4) Manage debt and transfers

Dedicated debt ledger and payments, plus easy transfers between accounts.

5) Sync and backup

Enable sync so everything stays safe if you change devices.

6) Security and privacy

App lock and local encryption protect your core data.
