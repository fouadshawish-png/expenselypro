Quick checks before you contact us

  • Update the app to the latest version on Google Play.
  • Confirm your internet connection and that you’re signed in.
  • Restart the app and try again if something is stuck.

Common fixes

  • Sync issues: re-login and ensure a stable connection.
  • Balance looks off: use “Recalculate balances” in Settings.
  • Ads not showing: update to the latest version.

Response time

We review messages daily and aim to reply within 24 hours. For urgent issues, include a concise description and screenshots.
