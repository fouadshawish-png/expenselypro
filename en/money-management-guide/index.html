Money Management Guide

This guide gives you a complete weekly money system: track expenses daily, plan budgets realistically, review weekly, and link every decision to goals and debt.

What money management really is

It’s an ongoing loop: log transactions daily, categorize, read weekly reports, then adjust budgets and debt decisions. Clarity first, decisions second.

Expenses vs. budget

ItemExpensesBudget
DefinitionWhat you already spentWhat you plan to spend
TimingAfter the transactionBefore the period starts
RoleDiagnose realityGuide decisions
Best outcomeClear spending patternLess chaos and drift

Why people fail with money

  • Relying on memory instead of daily tracking.
  • Perfect budgets that ignore reality.
  • No weekly 10-minute review.
  • Not linking spending to goals and debt.
  • Emotional decisions without data.

The weekly money system (4 steps)

  1. Log expenses immediately (keep categories lean).
  2. Set weekly caps from a realistic monthly budget.
  3. Run a 10-minute weekly review: top 3 categories, one fix.
  4. Protect savings/debt transfers—adjust wants, not goals.
