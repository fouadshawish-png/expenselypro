Cost of Living Expenses Guide

By Fouad Shawish · February 13, 2026

Living costs keep shifting. This guide shows how to map your essentials, set weekly caps, and keep a buffer so price spikes don’t wreck your month.

Build your living-cost map

  • Essentials: housing, utilities, groceries, transport.
  • Obligations: debt, subscriptions you must keep.
  • Buffer: 5–10% for inflation and surprises.

Convert to weekly limits

Divide monthly amounts by 4.3. Review weekly; adjust one category at a time.

Protect your buffer

Never pull from savings to cover lifestyle drift. First cut flexible spend, then reconsider obligations, savings last.
