Debt Control Strategy: Stop the Spiral, Then Accelerate Payoff

This playbook helps you stabilize debt payments, avoid new balances, and choose a clear payoff order.

Step 1: Freeze the bleed

  • Stop new debt: remove cards from wallets/apps; switch wants to cash.
  • Call issuers to move due dates after payday.
  • List all debts: balance, APR, minimum, due date.

Step 2: Build a starter buffer

Save $500–$1,000 first (see the emergency fund guide). It prevents new swipes for small shocks.

Step 3: Pick a payoff method

  • Debt avalanche: Highest APR first (fastest mathematically).
  • Debt snowball: Smallest balance first (fastest momentum).
  • Hybrid: If a small balance has a high APR, clear it first.

Step 4: Automate

Automate all minimums + one extra payment on the focus debt right after payday. Label transfers so you can’t re‑spend them.

Step 5: Weekly check‑in

  • Confirm payments posted.
  • Track total balance line—downward trend matters more than perfection.
  • Redirect any windfalls to the focus debt.

Step 6: When cash is tight

  • Ask for hardship plans or lower APR offers.
  • Pause one non‑essential subscription and move that amount to the focus debt.
  • Sell one item weekly until you free an extra payment.

Combine this with the budget guide and daily tracking to keep spending aligned with payoff speed.
