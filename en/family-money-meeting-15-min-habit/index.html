The 15-Minute Weekly Family Money Meeting

Financial stress often comes from misalignment. A short, structured weekly money meeting eliminates surprises and builds clarity.

Why Weekly, Not Monthly?

Shorter feedback loops prevent emotional escalation and hidden overspending.

The 15-Minute Structure

  • Review total weekly spending
  • Compare with planned weekly limits
  • Discuss one adjustment
  • Confirm priorities for next week

Rules for Success

Keep it short. Focus on numbers, not blame. Adjust calmly.

Result

Small weekly corrections prevent large monthly conflicts.
