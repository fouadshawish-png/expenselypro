Weekly Budget Review

Spend 10 minutes each week to keep your budget aligned with real spending and protect savings and debt payments.

The checklist

  1. Open reports: note top 3 categories this week.
  2. Mark any category over its weekly cap.
  3. Choose one correction for next week (lower cap, swap, or pause).
  4. Confirm savings/debt transfers remain untouched.

Why it works

  • Short feedback cycle prevents drift.
  • One change per week is sustainable.
  • Keeps goals funded while controlling lifestyle spend.

Set it up

  • Use weekly caps (monthly ÷ 4.3) for variable/flexible categories.
  • Schedule the review on the same day/time weekly.
  • Keep categories lean (8–10) for speed.
