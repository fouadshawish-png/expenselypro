Publishing Calendar

Monthly Operating Model

Week 1

  • Publish comprehensive guide #1 (2000+ words).
  • Publish supporting article #1.
  • Publish two social snippets (Snippet + Tip).

Week 2

  • Publish supporting article #2.
  • Update one high-traffic legacy article.
  • Publish two social snippets.

Week 3

  • Publish comprehensive guide #2 (2000+ words).
  • Publish supporting article #3.
  • Publish two social snippets.

Week 4

  • Publish supporting article #4.
  • Run the Search Console Loop.
  • Publish two social snippets.

60-Day Cadence

  • Month 1:
    • 2 guides + 4 articles + 8 social pieces.
  • Month 2:
    • 2 guides + 4 articles + 8 social pieces.
    • Refresh 5 strong articles (examples + FAQ + date).

KPI Targets

  • Average time on page for pillar articles: +20%.
  • Pages/session: +15%.
  • CTR on core keywords: +10%.
  • Conversion rate to download page: +12%.

Publishing Checklist (per content piece)

  • Clear SEO title.
  • Meta description of suitable length.
  • Internal links (Hub + Pillar + Sub-article + Download).
  • FAQ section.
  • Reading CTA + action CTA.
  • Relevant image with alt text.

Seasonal Publishing Windows

  • 3 weeks before Ramadan: Ramadan expense plan.
  • 2 weeks before Eid: seasonal spending control.
  • 2 weeks before back-to-school: school budget.
  • Start of year: financial reset plan.
  • End of year: annual financial review.
