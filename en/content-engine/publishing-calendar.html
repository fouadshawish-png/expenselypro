  • Publish supporting article #1.

  • Publish two social snippets.

Week 3

  • Publish comprehensive guide #2 (2000+ words).
  • Publish supporting article #3.
  • Publish two social snippets.

Week 4

  • Publish supporting article #4.
  • Run the Search Console Loop.

60-Day Cadence

  • Month 1:
    • 2 guides + 4 articles + 8 social pieces.
  • Month 2:

KPI Targets

  • Average time on page (pillar articles): +20%.
  • Pages per session: +15%.
  • CTR on core keywords: +10%.
  • Conversion rate to download page: +12%.

Publishing Checklist (Per Content Piece)

  • Clear SEO title.
  • Optimized meta description.
  • Internal links (Hub + Pillar + Sub-article + Download).
  • FAQ section included.
  • Reading CTA + action CTA.
  • Relevant image with descriptive alt text.

  • 2 weeks before Eid: Seasonal spending control plan.
  • 2 weeks before back-to-school: School budgeting framework.
  • Start of year: Financial reset blueprint.
  • End of year: Annual financial review guide.
