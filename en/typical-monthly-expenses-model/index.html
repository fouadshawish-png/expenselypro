Typical Monthly Expenses Model

Start with a four-bucket model, then convert to weekly caps. This keeps the plan realistic and prevents end-of-month drift.

The four buckets

  • Fixed: rent, utilities, insurance, debt minimums.
  • Variable essentials: groceries, transport, essentials that move with usage.
  • Flexible wants: dining, shopping, entertainment.
  • Goals: savings and extra debt payments.

Convert to weekly control

  1. Divide variable and flexible totals by 4.3 to get weekly caps.
  2. Log expenses daily; review top 3 categories weekly.
  3. Adjust one flexible category per week; never raid goals to cover drift.
