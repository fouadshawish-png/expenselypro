Start your money routine in three moves

Set a budget, track expenses, and review debt weekly. In under 15 minutes per week you’ll keep spending controlled and decisions straightforward.

1) Create your first wallet or account

  • Open Accounts → Add new account.
  • Select type (cash, bank, card).
  • Enter an accurate opening balance.

2) Add your first transaction

  • Tap Add transaction.
  • Choose type: Expense / Income / Debt.
  • Pick the right account, amount, category, and date.

3) Manage debt

  • Go to Debt → Add creditor/borrower.
  • Record the outstanding amount and each payment.

4) Run the weekly review

  • Open Reports → Weekly review.
  • Check over/under categories and adjust next week’s limits.
  • Confirm debt payments and update goals.
