Who Is Fouad Shawish?

Fouad Shawish is the developer behind Expensely Pro, a structured weekly money system designed to simplify personal finance without complexity. His focus is not on generic financial advice — but on building a practical system people can actually follow daily.

Specialization in Expense Management and Budget Systems

Fouad’s core expertise is simplifying expense tracking, structuring monthly budgets, and turning financial data into practical decisions. The emphasis is always on real-world execution: daily logging, weekly review, and connecting spending behavior to savings and debt goals.

Why Expensely Pro Was Created

Many people understand budgeting in theory — but struggle with daily implementation. Expensely Pro was built to close that gap by integrating expense tracking, budgeting, reporting, and debt monitoring into one structured workflow.

The Problem the System Solves

The main issue in personal finance is not lack of information. It is lack of structure. Most users know they should track expenses and follow a budget — but they do not have a clear daily process. The system connects logging, reviewing, analyzing, and deciding into one simple weekly cycle.

Applied Experience in Personal Finance Systems

This expertise comes from building and refining a real financial product based on actual usage behavior. The focus is behavioral: how users stay consistent, how numbers translate into decisions, and how to create a financial system that lasts long term instead of collapsing after a few weeks.

Vision: Structured Financial Culture

The core vision is to promote a practical financial culture based on structure and consistency rather than short-term motivation. The goal is to help users build a clear weekly system for managing expenses and improving financial decisions gradually.

The philosophy is simple: Run your money weekly. Stay in control daily.
