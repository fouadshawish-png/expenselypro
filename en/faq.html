Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Sync

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings → Backup → Enable automatic sync with Google Drive. Your data will be securely encrypted and saved.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes. Sign in with the same Google account on all devices and your data will sync automatically.

How do I upgrade to Business?

Go to More → Upgrade Account → Choose Business plan to unlock advanced features and priority support.

Expenses & Income

How do I add an expense or income?

Tap the center + button in the bottom navigation, choose Expense or Income, enter details, then save.

How do I edit or delete a transaction?

Open the transaction from the main screen and modify or delete it from the edit page.

Can I attach a receipt image?

Yes. While adding an expense, scroll down and tap Attach Image to capture or select a receipt photo.

Debt System

What is the difference between "Debt Owed to Me" and "Debt I Owe"?

"Owed to Me" means money someone owes you. "I Owe" means money you borrowed and must repay.

How do I add a new debt?

Tap + → Debt → Select type → Enter name, amount, and date.

Accounts & Cards

How do I add a new bank account?

Go to Accounts → + → Choose type → Enter opening balance and currency.

Can I manage multiple currencies?

Yes. Create separate accounts for each currency.

Reports & Analytics

How do I view the monthly report?

Go to Reports → Monthly Report → Select month and year.

Can I export reports?

Yes. Open a report and tap the export/share icon to export as PDF or Excel.

Security & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes. All backups are end-to-end encrypted before uploading to Firebase.

How do I enable biometric lock?

Go to Settings → Security → Enable fingerprint or face unlock.

Subscriptions

What is included in Business?

Advanced AI reports, team management, auto exports, secure vault, and VIP support.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Manage your subscription from Google Play settings.
