Weekly Budget Review That Prevents Financial Collapse

A 10-minute weekly review that shows where cash is leaking, so you can adjust before the month falls apart.

Open reports (web) Open reports (app)

Why a weekly review?

It catches overspending before it snowballs. Four weeks of small leaks create the “end-of-month crash.” One weekly check prevents it.

10-minute routine

  1. Open reports, note total spend and buffer left.
  2. List top 3 categories over plan.
  3. Choose one corrective action (cap, delay, swap).
  4. Move any surplus to debt/emergency fund immediately.

Fast rules

  • If buffer < 5% of income → freeze wants for 3 days.
  • Any want over $25 waits 24h.
  • Dining out max once a week until buffer recovers.

Example

  • Spend vs plan: +$90 over.
  • Top leak: dining +$60, delivery +$30.
  • Fix: cook 3 dinners, pause delivery; move $40 from wants to buffer.

FAQ

What if I miss a week? Do a quick catch-up (5 minutes): totals, top leak, one fix. Resume next week.

How detailed must reports be? Broad categories are enough; speed matters more.

How to keep it to 10 minutes? Pre-set the three questions: totals, top leaks, one action.
