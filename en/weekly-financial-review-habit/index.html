Weekly Financial Review Habit (20 Minutes)

Use this 20-minute flow to keep budgets, goals, and debt aligned every week.

The 20-minute flow

  1. Open reports: top 5 categories and weekly totals.
  2. Spot overspends; pick one category to correct next week.
  3. Confirm savings/debt transfers are funded.
  4. Update one goal metric (e.g., emergency fund %, debt balance).
  5. Note one action for next week (cap, pause, or swap).

Why it matters

Weekly visibility prevents month-end surprises and keeps motivation steady because progress is visible every seven days.
