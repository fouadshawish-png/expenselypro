Types of Expenses: The 4-Bucket Model

By Fouad Shawish · February 13, 2026

The 4 buckets

  • Fixed: rent, utilities, insurance, debt minimums.
  • Variable essentials: groceries, transport, essentials that move with usage.
  • Flexible wants: dining, shopping, entertainment.
  • Goals: savings, sinking funds, extra debt payments.

How to use the buckets

  • Set monthly amounts, then convert variable/flexible to weekly caps.
  • Protect goals by funding them on day one (auto-transfer).
  • During weekly review, adjust only flexible wants unless a fixed bill changed.

Why this simplifies budgeting

Too many categories slow logging and hide priorities. Four buckets keep the plan clear and enforce weekly corrections.
