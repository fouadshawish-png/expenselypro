Fast Debt Repayment: Clear Your Balances in 90 Days

By Fouad Shawish · January 23, 2026

Open reports (web) Open reports (in app)

Fast repayment is about consistency, not hero payments. This 90-day plan fixes a strong monthly amount, adds small weekly boosts, and snowballs every closed debt into the next one.

Step 1: Prioritize debts

List every balance in Debt and sort by:

  1. Highest cost (rate/fees)
  2. Past-due or near due
  3. Lowest cost

Step 2: Fix a strong monthly payment

In your budget, lock a monthly payment above all minimums.

  • Minimums total: $1,200
  • Extra fixed: $500

Total monthly debt payment: $1,700. This is the engine of acceleration.

Step 3: Add weekly accelerators

  • Add any weekly savings to the debt line.
  • Route side income at a fixed % to debt (e.g., 40%).
  • When a debt closes, roll its full payment to the next balance.

Example timeline

Balances: $12,000 total. Monthly engine: $1,700. Weekly boosters: ~$75. Result: you shave multiple months off compared to minimums.

Keep the plan steady

  • Review debt progress in the 10-minute weekly review.
  • Do not pull from savings goals; trim flexible spending first.
  • If cash is tight one week, reduce only non-essentials—never skip the fixed payment.

FAQ

Snowball or avalanche? Prioritize high cost (avalanche) unless motivation is an issue—then start with a small balance first, but switch back to cost order.

Variable income? Base the fixed payment on your lowest reliable month; apply extra when income is higher.
