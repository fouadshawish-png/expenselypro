Advanced Features: An Actionable Checklist to Tighten Expenses and Grow Savings

1. Smart Analytics

Advanced analytics help you understand spending patterns, identify waste, and make confident financial decisions based on real data — not guesswork.

2. Salary Cycle Management

Set your own salary cycle start and end dates. All reports and insights are calculated based on your actual income cycle instead of the standard calendar month.

3. Multi-Account Management

Manage multiple account types including cash wallets, bank accounts, and cards. Easily transfer funds between accounts while keeping full visibility.

4. Advanced Debt Tracking

Track debts per person with a complete transaction history and automatic balance updates so you always know what remains.

5. Sync & Backup

Enable secure cloud sync to restore your data when switching devices. Your privacy is protected — no data is sold or shared with third parties.
