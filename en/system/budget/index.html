Realistic Budget Framework

The realistic budget is the third pillar. Without it, tracking and review have no clear target.

Why a Realistic Budget Matters

  • Gives you a clear plan for your income.
  • Prevents running out of money before month-end.
  • Makes weekly decisions more precise.

How to Build a Realistic Budget

  1. Gather real spending data.
  2. Set your monthly priorities.
  3. Break the budget into weekly blocks.
