Debt Control Strategy

Debt control is the fourth pillar. Without a clear plan for debt, even the best budget can collapse.

Why Debt Control Matters

  • Prevents interest and financial stress from piling up.
  • Gives you peace of mind and stability.
  • Helps you move forward toward your goals.

How to Control Debt

  1. List all debts accurately.
  2. Create a weekly or monthly repayment plan.
  3. Track your progress and adjust as needed.
