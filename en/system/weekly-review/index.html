Weekly Financial Review

The weekly review is the second pillar. It gives you a chance to correct your course before the month ends.

Why Weekly Review Matters

  • Reduces financial drift early.
  • Gives you clarity on real performance.
  • Helps you adjust your plan quickly.

How to Run a Weekly Review

  1. Set aside 20 minutes each week to review your spending.
  2. Compare your budget to actual spending.
  3. Make a corrective decision immediately.
