Daily Expense Tracking

Tracking your expenses daily is the first pillar of real financial control. Without it, you can't build a budget or correct your course.

Why Expense Tracking Matters

  • Reveals hidden leaks before they grow.
  • Shows exactly where your money goes.
  • Enables weekly, actionable decisions.

How to Track Expenses

  1. Log every transaction within 24 hours.
  2. Use an app or a simple notebook.
  3. Review your weekly total every Friday.
