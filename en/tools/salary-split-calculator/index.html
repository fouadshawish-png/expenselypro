Salary Split Calculator

This calculator helps you turn your monthly income into a clear, usable structure in less than a minute. Instead of guessing where your salary goes, you enter your income, savings percentage, and essential expense percentage, then instantly see how much remains for flexible spending. The goal is not a rigid ratio. The goal is a split you can sustain week after week. Once your numbers are visible, decision-making becomes calmer: you know what can be spent, what must be protected, and where to adjust before the month gets out of control. Use this as your starting framework, then refine the percentages with weekly review.

FAQ

Do I need to use fixed percentages every month?

No. Keep the structure, then adjust percentages based on real conditions and obligations.

What is a realistic minimum savings ratio?

Start with 10% if possible. Consistency matters more than a large number at the beginning.

What if my essentials are too high?

Use the result to see the gap clearly, then reduce flexible categories or optimize recurring bills.

How do I maintain this split?

Run a short weekly review and make one small adjustment at a time.
