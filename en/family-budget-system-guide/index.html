Family Budget System Guide

Create a simple weekly family budget that everyone can follow. Agree on rules, review weekly, and protect savings and debt first.

Set shared rules

  • Essentials funded first (housing, food, transport).
  • Savings/debt transfers automated on payday.
  • Weekly caps for flexible spending.
  • One discretionary buffer per week.

Weekly family review (15 minutes)

  1. Top 3 spend categories this week.
  2. One adjustment for next week.
  3. Confirm goals and debt stayed funded.
  4. Note any upcoming expenses.
