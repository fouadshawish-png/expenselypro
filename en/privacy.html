Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-26

1. Overview

Expensely Pro ("we", "our", or "the App") is a personal financial management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency regarding how data is handled.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

If you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may access your name, email address, and basic profile information strictly for authentication purposes.

Financial Data

All financial entries (expenses, income, debts, notes) are stored locally on your device. If cloud synchronization or backup is enabled, data may be stored securely using Firebase services.

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. This helps us understand feature usage and improve performance. No personally identifiable financial details are shared for analytics purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Data stored in Firebase services is protected by industry-standard security practices, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS). Access to cloud data is restricted to authenticated users.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal financial information. Third-party services such as Google Firebase and Analytics process limited data solely to provide authentication, hosting, and analytics functionality.

5. User Control

You may delete your account and associated cloud data at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Expensely Pro is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@expenselypro.com
